NYSOH Production Incident Report
Date: 2024-01-15
Severity: High
Module: PaymentService

Description:
Users are experiencing payment failures when processing
credit card transactions. The error occurs specifically
during the authorization step of the payment flow.
Error logs show NullPointerException at line 247 of
PaymentService.java in the processTransaction method.

Impact:
- 847 failed transactions since 2:47 AM
- Estimated revenue impact: $42,000
- Affects all credit card payment types
- Debit cards working normally

Timeline:
- 2:30 AM: Deployment CR-4821 pushed to production
- 2:47 AM: First error reported in logs
- 3:15 AM: Alert triggered, on-call team notified
- 3:45 AM: Investigation started

Initial Findings:
The error stack trace points to a null check that was
removed in CR-4821. The paymentMethod object is not
being validated before processTransaction() is called.
The fix requires restoring the null check with proper
Optional wrapper pattern.
